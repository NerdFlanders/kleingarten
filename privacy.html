Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2024

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Apply for an allotment plot
  • Register for events or workshops
  • Contact us with questions or feedback
  • Subscribe to our newsletter

This may include your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your allotment applications and manage plot assignments
  • Send you important updates about garden activities and events
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our services and community programs
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy. We may share your information:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating our allotment garden
  • When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

We regularly review our data collection, storage, and processing practices to ensure the security of your personal information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of the information we have about you
  • File a complaint with relevant data protection authorities

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These technologies help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve our website's functionality and performance

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.
